Bridgeland stability seminar

Introduction to triangulated categories

In this talk, a quick introduction to triangulated categories is given. The main reference is Huybrechts' book.
  • Definition of additive categories and subcategories. Full subcategories. k-linearity. Fully faithful functors, essentially surjective functors. Equivalences.
  • Definition of abelian category. Left- (right-) exact functors. Injectives and projectives.
  • Triangulated structure on an additive category: distinguished triangles, shifts and (a sketch of) the four axioms. Triangulated subcategories.
